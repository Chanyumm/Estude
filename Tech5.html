Take Notes

As much as it is kind of a hassle, taking notes is very helpful since it helps you remember important things that your instructor said and it tends to retain in your mind better. The reasoning behind this is because makes you concentrate and listen since you are selecting the different things that needs to be written down which also makes you understand the lesson easier. Additionally, these would serve as reviewers when there is an incoming high-scored test or simply just for a short and simple reminder when you forgot.
